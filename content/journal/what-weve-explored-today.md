## What we explored
- Moved to a future-proof `src/` structure.
- Kept the site fully vanilla with no Node dependency.
- Discussed long-term maintainability and standards.

## Decisions made
- Keep root HTML pages as entry points.
- Keep source JS/CSS/content under `src/`.
- Use markdown files for journal entries with a manifest for discovery.

## Why this works
This keeps your workflow simple now, while giving you a clean path to add tooling later if you choose.
